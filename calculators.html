Impact Calculator

Discover The Impact Of Your Security Awareness Training

What impact is your security awareness training program having? What could it achieve with a new and improved approach that automates more frequent, personalized, and realistic training?

Traditional SAT programs are hindered by:

  • Low engagement: Since training is only provided to those who fail, it reaches only a small percentage of users.
  • Misleading metrics: One-size-fits-all simulations are either too hard or too easy for most users.
  • Infrequent delivery: Creating and deploying simulations is time-consuming.
